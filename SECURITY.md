# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Do not open a public GitHub issue.** Use one of these channels:

1. **GitHub private vulnerability reporting** (preferred) — go to the
   affected repo's `Security` tab and click "Report a vulnerability".
   This creates a private advisory only the maintainers can see.
2. **Email:** info@esegments.com — please include "SECURITY" in the
   subject line. We may ask you to move to a private GitHub advisory
   for tracking.

## What to include

- Affected repo and version (commit SHA if from main).
- Reproduction steps. A minimal proof-of-concept helps.
- Expected vs. actual behavior.
- Your assessment of severity and impact.

## Our process

- We'll acknowledge receipt within **3 business days**.
- We'll evaluate and respond with a triage decision within **7 business days**.
- For confirmed issues we'll coordinate a fix and disclosure timeline
  with you. Default disclosure window is **90 days** unless you and we
  agree otherwise.
- We credit reporters in the released advisory unless they request
  anonymity.

## Scope

This policy applies to all repos under the
[JungleMangal organization](https://github.com/JungleMangal).
Out of scope: third-party libraries we depend on (report those
upstream), social engineering of staff, denial of service via
infrastructure abuse.

## Safe harbor

Good-faith security research that follows this policy is welcome.
We will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

- Stay within the scope above.
- Do not access, modify, or delete other users' data.
- Give us reasonable time to fix issues before public disclosure.
