# Code of Conduct

E-Segments projects follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## TL;DR

- **Be respectful.** Disagree with ideas, not people.
- **Be constructive.** Critique should help; venting belongs elsewhere.
- **No harassment.** Discrimination, sexualized language, doxxing,
  trolling, or sustained personal attacks are not welcome.
- **Assume good faith.** Most missteps are accidental. Ask before
  escalating.

## Reporting

Report violations to **info@esegments.com**. We read every report.
Confidentiality is protected to the extent possible while we
investigate.

## Scope

Applies in all project spaces — issues, PRs, discussions, code review,
and any official communication channel.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, or contributions that
violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban repeat
offenders. Decisions are final but appealable to info@esegments.com.
